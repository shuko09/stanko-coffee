Discover the creative freedom STANKO offers to individuals

At STANKO, they really care about making sure everyone feels right at home when they're visiting the shop. It doesn't matter who you are or where you come from – Their doors are open for you to grab a coffee and relax. STANKO is doing their bit to help build a society that's all about being equal and respectful, where nobody gets left out or treated unfairly.

STANKO comes from the word “stanko”, the Surinamese word for friend. The most famous synonym is the word “Mattie” STANKO COFFEE refers to not only a place where everyone is welcome and accepted but also a place to meet like minded people.

STANKO is all about supporting artists and creative groups by teaming up in different ways. Ceramics, fashion, and all sorts of cool things. The art STANKO has on display switches up every couple of months. This means you'll always get to see something fresh from a new visual artist around Amsterdam and nearby places.

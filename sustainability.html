COFFEE

STANKO offers all types of coffees. They work closely with Back to Black Coffee and their green bean importers. The Back to Black importers roast their own coffee and have been in the business for a very long time. They do not only have their own shops, but they also offer a variety of services including coffee memberships to individuals but also offer other specialty coffee shops (not the kind you’re thinking of) to sell ethically sourced coffee. They hand select the unroasted coffee bean importers and stimulate to bring knowledge to the coffee bean farmers on how to work more ethically correct; Higher prices for labor &. the best ways to store the coffee. The importers they work closely with are: This Side Up & Cultivar.

SNACKS

The snacks that STANKO offers are carefully selected by and with the family of the two owners; Kick van Doorn and Nicole Lieuw. They sell delicious Surinamese pastries and other delicious foreign foods. They offer a special order service for individuals to buy in “bulk”, like a bag of cookies instead of just one, a whole loaf of cake instead of just one slice or a whole pie instead of just one piece.
